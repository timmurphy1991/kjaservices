Our Process

01

Design Stage

At KJA Services, the design stage is handled with precision and care. We refine your brief, assess the space, and develop a considered solution tailored to your requirements. Each proposal includes detailed drawings, a transparent quotation, and a clear timeline.

02

Manufacturing Stage

Skilled craftsmen transform high grade steel into precision made Crittall doors, screens, and windows. Each frame is cut, welded, galvanised, and powder coated for durability, then rigorously quality checked to ensure a lasting finish.

03

Installation Stage

The installation stage brings your Crittall design to life. Our experienced team ensures a precise, secure fit with careful attention to detail throughout. We work efficiently and respectfully, leaving your space clean and ready to enjoy.

Our Services

Supply Only Service

We supply all Crittall products (excluding fire-rated) on a supply only basis for you or your builder to install. Once your order enters production, we provide glass sizes for you to source. Delivery is free across mainland GB, or collection is available from our Barnsley, South Yorkshire warehouse.

Supply & Installation Service

At KJA Services, we take pride in the installation of all Crittall products. As a high quality, design led product, we ensure our installation reflects the same standard. Our experienced team is extensively trained to deliver precise, reliable installations every time.

Renovation Service

We offer a renovation service for all Crittall products, subject to condition and suitability. Our process includes careful cleaning, rust treatment, and repainting to restore your doors, screens and windows. Please contact us with photos of the items you would like to renovate.
